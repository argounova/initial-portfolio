About

Welcome and thanks for stopping by! Here you will find examples of my most recent work, but first, allow me to introduce myself. I am a full stack JavaScript web developer and simply put, I build web applications. Recently graduated from an in-depth MERN bootcamp at the University of Central Florida, I've been supremely focused on advancing my understanding of everything from HTML and CSS to MongoDB, mySQL, React and Express/Node.js.

Originally from Colorado, I currently live off the western coast of Florida about an hour north of Tampa Bay. I enjoy exploring Florida, cooking and spending time with family, friends and my pets. When I'm not in front of the computer, you can typically find me in the garage tinkering with one of my cars. I have a passion for motorsports and enjoy NASA track days at Sebring as often as time allows.

Cars and computers go hand in hand these days and in the not-so-distant-future, cars will be computers. As a developer with a wealth of automotive knowledge, I seek to position myself at the forefront of such an intersection. I hope to work with innovative, free-thinking, dedicated and diverse individuals who hold progress and global awareness as some of their core values. Ready to get started? I'd love to hear from you.
